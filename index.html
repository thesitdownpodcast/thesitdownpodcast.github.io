About the Sitdown

The Sitdown is where Aka Omg the host and producer sit down with a new guest every week to discuss various topics. In the show The Sitdown we will be learning life lessons, wisdom, and knowledge from not necessarily experts, but from real people that are relatable and familiar. Aka Omg hopes that from these sit-downs it will highlight the beauty of conversation and the power that it has to spread across communities and lend a helping hand. You are never alone in any situation, but if you feel this way or maybe just want some extra company the come sit down with us.
